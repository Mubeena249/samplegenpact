Private equity

Enterprise value – delivered.

Turbocharge value creation for your portfolio

The private equity landscape is changing. Regulatory pressures are increasing. Environmental, social, and governance initiatives are taking a front seat. Market dynamics are evolving. And advanced digital technologies are causing disruption. So private equity firms must widen their scope to look for new sources of value. But increasing business value is not only about making short-term improvements to the top and bottom line. It's about creating long-term value in a sustainable and transparent way.

Genpact partners with private equity firms across their investment life cycle to accelerate value creation. We improve the business outcomes of your portfolio companies by converting their data into actionable insights and transforming their operations across front, middle, and back offices. Our private equity consulting teams help investors craft effective, high-yield strategies that improve your portfolio companies' performance. Our Lean Digital℠ methodology enables companies with complex, pre-existing, and often dated operations to embrace the art of the possible. And our outcome-based commercial models measure value against critical business goals, such as revenue growth and working capital optimization – giving you a high level of confidence in our mutual success.

From functional improvements to enterprise-wide transformations – we do it all. What's more, we adapt our approach to each client's needs. With more than 20 years of industry, business, and operational expertise – and backed by our extensive partner ecosystem – we help portfolio companies improve growth, increase cash flow, and reduce costs.

Our impact

The continuous transformation framework

The best way to create sustainable, long-term value is to make business transformation continuous.


Drive revenue growth

We enable portfolio companies to drive revenue growth, enhance profitability, and gain a competitive edge in the market by reducing revenue leakage and optimizing pricing, promotions, and channel dynamics.
Sales efficiency and effectiveness

Streamline your sales processes, improve resource allocation, and drive better sales outcomes with our end-to-end sales operation services. We combine consultancy, advanced digital technologies, analytics , and managed services to help your portfolio companies build a world-class, omnichannel sales force supported by integrated, cross-functional teams.

Customer success and lifetime value

Boost the customer retention rates of your portfolio companies by giving them a 360-degree view of their customers. Through customer journey mapping, process redesign, technology implementation, and personalized marketing strategies and campaigns, Genpact helps companies maximize customer lifetime value and drive customer loyalty.

Digital marketing

Improve customer engagement, enhance marketing effectiveness, and drive revenue growth with our end-to-end digital marketing solutions, which are underpinned by data, analytics, and an ecosystem of AI-driven marketing technologies partners. We help companies find, win, and renew more new business to maximize top-line marketing impact and return on investment.

Reduce costs

We empower portfolio companies to enhance profitability and financial performance by improving cost structures and reducing costs.
Target operating model

Streamline organizational structure, processes, and geographic models to drive operational efficiency and cost savings. Genpact helps identify optimal locations for cost-effective service delivery, leading to enhanced performance and reduced expenses.

Process intelligence

Increase productivity by implementing process improvements and eliminating bottlenecks. Genpact's expertise ensures streamlined processes and higher resource utilization. We bring our industry, technology, and data expertise together to deliver a combined value that's greater than the sum of its parts.

Intelligent automation

Improve the speed and accuracy of your portfolio companies' business processes using our AI-driven automation solutions. Genpact enables businesses to harness advanced digital technologies to optimize their processes for sustainable improvements.

Technology services

Modernize your portfolio companies' IT infrastructure, accelerate their journey to cloud, and reduce their hardware and IT maintenance costs. Genpact's IT expertise empowers you to make the most of the technology you've already invested in and introduce advanced technologies, such as generative AI, to improve speed, accuracy, and customer satisfaction.

Improve working capital

Maximize your cash impact and improve your financial ratios. We help companies manage working capital to support their long-term goals by addressing receivables, inventory, and payables.
